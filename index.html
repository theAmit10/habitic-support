// FREQUENTLY ASKED

No. Habitic runs entirely on your device — there's no server to reach, so it works the same in airplane mode as it does anywhere else. Reminders, streaks, and check-ins all happen locally.

Open Settings → Export on your old phone to save a single backup file. Install Habitic on the new phone and use Settings → Import to load that same file — every habit and streak comes with it.

You choose the location when you export — Files, iCloud Drive, a messaging app, or anywhere else your phone lets you save to. Habitic doesn't store a copy anywhere else, so keep it somewhere you'll find again.

No, and we're upfront about why: since nothing is ever sent to a server, we have no copy to restore from. This is the tradeoff of being fully offline — export regularly if your history matters to you.

No account, no cloud sync, and no subscription tied to your data. Habitic stores everything locally by design — that's not a limited "offline mode," it's the whole app.

Swipe left on any habit and choose Delete to remove just that one. To clear all data, go to Settings → Reset — export a backup first if you might want it later.
